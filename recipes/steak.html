Steak

Steak is one of the greatest foods of all time in my opinion. A perfectly made steak is both juicy and tender to prefection. In this recipe, I will teach you how to make perfectly medium - medium rare steak.

Ingredients:

  • A piece of good quality steak meat, around 1 inches thick
  • 2 teaspoon peanut oil
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • pepper (as much as you want)
  • garlic and rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Take uncooked steak meat out of fridge and add salt and pepper to both sides of the meat equally. Let meat sit at room temperature for 20-30 mins.
  2. Put pan on stove (if you want good crust dont use non-stick pans), add oil, and heat stove to medium high - high heat. let oil heat up for 2-3 mins.
  3. add steak to pan once oil is heated up. Let steak sit in pan untouched for 1 minute. Flip steak and allow steak to cook undisturbed for another minute.
  4. Once steak is finished cooking, turn off heat, take pan off heat, and add butter, garlic, and rosemary into pan. Use spoon to baste melted butter onto steak for another minute.
  5. Take steak out of pan, and leave at room temperature for 5-10 minutes before eating.
  6. Enjoy!
